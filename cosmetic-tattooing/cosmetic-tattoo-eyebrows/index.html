Eyebrows

What is cosmetic tattooing of the eyebrows?

Eyebrows not only help frame our facial features, but are essential to expressing our emotions. It’s therefore no surprise that your appearance can be drastically changed by the alteration of your eyebrows.

The size, length and thickness is heavily dependant on personal preference, but there are cultural norms which influence what we perceive to be beautiful. An individual’s eyebrows frame their eyes and give character to their face. We don’t realise how much communication and expression there is in the eyes and the eyebrows till they are hidden or altered.

Before and after eyebrow feathering.

Who’s cosmetic tattooing of the eyebrows for?

Individuals who have thinned out their eyebrows from overzealous plucking, or who have seen them become sparse as a product of ageing, can achieve very convincing results through semi–permanent makeup. Soft thin lines can be achieved using a slight variation in colours, creating a very pleasing result. Eyebrows can be filled out if they lack natural hair, or can be extended if they have lost some length. Eyebrows can also be reshaped. Semi-permanent eyebrows makeup is a preferred cosmetic procedure, recommended for people who:

  • Have thin eyebrows.
  • Have thick eyebrows.
  • Have asymmetrical eyebrows.
  • Have partially missing eyebrows, or scarring that affects eyebrow growth.
  • Busy individuals who do not have time for makeup application, or have minimal makeup skills.
  • Have an oily skin type that does not hold makeup.
  • Enjoy sports or activities where sweat or action does not allow for makeup to last any
  • Have scars/patches in their eyebrows.

What’s the procedure for cosmetic eyebrow tattooing?

Our cosmetic tattoo specialist will consult with you on desired eyebrow shape and colour. Please make a note of any questions you have and the type of result you desire. We do not wish to leave anything to chance.

Semi-Permanent eyebrow makeup will fade slightly after the first few days, so the initial application is made deliberately darker than the final result. As every individual situation is different, a touch-up session might be required. Our consultants will advise what is best for you.

There are 3 major types of semi-permanent eyebrows makeup. The most common procedure for semi-permanent eyebrow makeup is called Shadowing or Blocking. These procedures are suitable for those who have unbalanced or thinning eyebrows, and can be removed naturally without smudging.

Also popular is Embroidery eyebrows, otherwise known as 3D, Micro Blading or Feathering. This gives a natural looking and volumetric result by adding individual lines as well as the original eyebrow line. It is recommended for those who have dark coloured and thick eyebrows, often men, with gaps that need to be filled. The initial, natural look this achieves may fade overtime, meaning expert application and a possible touch-up may be required.

A combination of both Shadowing and Embroidery allows your eyebrows to have a balanced and shaped look. It feathers out the thinning edge to blend nicely with the original eyebrow line. This procedure is highly recommended because of the natural look and stable results.

Is there any downtime?

There can be a little redness on the day of the procedure, much like getting a regular tattoo. It’s important to follow strict aftercare for the best results.

Aftercare

  • Do not let the eyebrows come into contact with water for at least 24 hours
  • Apply soothing cream twice daily for a week
  • Avoid sun
  • Avoid swimming pools
  • The scabbing process will start around the third day, please do not pick at the scab
