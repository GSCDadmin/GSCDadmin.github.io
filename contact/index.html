How to get here

By Train

From Central Station take the T2 Airport, Inner West and South Line from platform 23 and get off at Green Square Station. It is only one stop from Central Station. Walk along Bourke Street in a city-bound direction for 5 minutes and you will arrive at Green Square Health. It is opposite the Kennards Self Storage building on the left hand side of Bourke Street.

By Car

The trip will take about 10 minutes from the city centre. Head in the direction of Cleveland Street, Surry Hills. Turn right onto Bourke Street from Cleveland Street and continue along past McEvoy Street. Green Square Health is on the right at the intersection of Powell and Bourke Streets.

By Bus

From Town Hall Station you can catch bus number M20 or 343. Bus number M20 leaves from Park Street (near Pitt Street) and takes about 20 minutes. Bus number 343 leaves from Castlereagh Street (near Bathurst Street) and takes about 22 minutes. Get off bus number 343 on Elizabeth Street near Bourke Street. From Central Station walk to the bus stop on Elizabeth Street (near Kippax Street in Surry Hills) and take bus number 343. The trip takes about 13 minutes. From Bondi Junction Station you can catch bus number 355. Get off on Phillip Street and walk south to Bourke Street. The trip takes about 30 minutes. For maps and travel information go to the Transport Info website. For detailed bus route maps go to the Sydney Buses website and select a route number from the dropdown menu.
