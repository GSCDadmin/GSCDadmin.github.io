Eyeliner

What is it?

Eyeliner semi-permanent makeup is good for people that have little time for applying makeup, or have a sporting career/lifestyle or any other factor that prevents the conventional makeup from staying on. People who have had surgery can also benefit greatly from the semi-permanent makeup.

Who’s it for?

Semi-permanent makeup is a preferred cosmetic procedure, recommended for those who:

  • Have dull looking eyes
  • Have oily skin
  • Enjoys sport
  • Are not good at makeup
  • Recently had double eyelid surgery
  • Have a small gap between their eyes

What’s the procedure?

Eyeliner semi-permanent makeup is good for covering flaws around your eyes and enhancing your natural beauty. Eye makeup can be very effective, but nothing is completely smudge proof. Even in the case of longer lasting products, the correct application is challenging. Semi-permanent eyeliner can be applied in varying degrees; far thinner than regular cosmetics or more boldly if a stronger impact is required. Different techniques, such as dotting between lashes, can be effective but subtle; strong lines can be used for intensity, or to change the apparent shape of the eye.

A deep black eye line fills gaps between inner lashes which enhances volume and provides clarity to your vision. The thickness of a line is decided during consultation with the client.
