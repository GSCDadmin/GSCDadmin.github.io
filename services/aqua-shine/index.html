Aqua-shine

Hydration straight into your skin

Aqua-shine is one of our most popular treatments, and for good reason. It makes you look better, by improving the actual quality of your skin. This isnâ€™t changing your shape, or stopping new wrinkles, it is just doing what we try to do every day, moisturising our skin.

What is it?

We use hyaluronic-acid, a gel that is already in our skin which holds all our cells together, and we place it in the layers that require boosting. This acts like a sponge, by soaking up and attracting even more water molecules to the skin giving you that young, moisturised and plump looking skin.

The product we use also contains 3 powerful anti-oxidants, zinc, copper and vitamin B6, as well as 8 amino acids.
