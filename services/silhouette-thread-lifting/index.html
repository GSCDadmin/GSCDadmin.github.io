Silhouette thread lifting

A 21st century lift, without surgery

The infamous facelift. We see some dramatic transformations in many Hollywood stars and starlets and wonder… why? An unnatural tension and a complete change in facial shape. It can boggle the mind. Thankfully this is a thing of the past (we can only hope). With the tools are our disposal there’s no need to undergo the knife for a lift that’s subtle and instantaneous. Cue the Silhouette Soft threadlift!

2 weeks post procedure, marionette lines and jowl improvement. There will be continual improvement up to 3 months, then maintenance for 18 months.

What is it?

Using advanced Poly L-Lactic Acid (PLLA) technology, which has been used for a long time in major surgical procedures as dissolvable sutures, we can achieve an instant, long lasting lift. These threads contain small cones that pull up the tissue that holds all our muscles and fat on our face, and stimulate collagen to strengthen this effect whilst improving the skin.

When planned with a doctor experienced in silhouette soft, the lift is not only instant, but achieves effects that are difficult with almost any other technology except invasive surgery. The effects typically then improve over 3 months even more due to the increase collagen, and last on average 18 months. However when mixed with dermal fillers, PRP or energy based devices, the effect can last up to 3 years.

Who’s it for?

People who have lost volume and are beginning to see the signs of ageing, particularly sagging cheeks, increased jowls and loss of that jawline definition. It really makes sense if you want to reverse the clock, but aren’t able to undergo an operation either due to the downtime/recovery, or prefer to delay a procedure until necessary.

It can also be used to tighten the neckline, or give a significant lift to droopy eyebrows/eyelids.

What’s the procedure?

Planning with your doctor is the first step. We simulate the lift by pulling on the skin where we’d like to place the threads. This gives us some idea of the initial lift we can achieve. We need to decide how many threads are required, and the length of these. After preparing and cleaning the area, local anaesthetic is used to numb the entry and exit points, and the threads are placed carefully under the skin. This is actually painless as the layer the threads lie in has no pain receptors.

Once all threads are placed, we tighten the threads and skin, clip the ends and admire the results!

Follow up is in a few days, then again in 1 month to marvel at the transformation we’ve just achieved.

Is there downtime?

There is often a small dimple created where the threads have entered the skin, and a small bruise. Both of these begin to improve within a week, and are often gone by 2 weeks. There is some post procedure swelling which often makes the lift look more impressive, but again this settles in a week or so to see the real effect. You can however go on with your day as per normal.

We ask that patients avoid excessive facial movements and gestures for 2 weeks, such as eating things that need the mouth stretched (baguettes), tough steak, or having big belly laughs. Sleeping propped up on an extra pillow also helps.
