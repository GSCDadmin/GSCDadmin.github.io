Skin brightening

A common request from our patients is to make their skin fairer (except for our patients who love a tan). And having travelled and attended skin conferences in Asia, the demand for such products are very high.

But like all good things, there are no shortcuts. It’s important we look at, and examine each of our patients’ skin very closely and get a full history to understand the reasoning for their darker pigmentation.

Like treating melasma, general freckles, sun spots, age spots and acne scars can be treated with similar oral and topical medication to improve colour and texture. Lightening of the skin should be seen within four weeks.

But skin is more than just its appearance, as it serves a vital function. And healthy functioning skin, is healthy looking skin. So using our aqua-shine and PRP treatments to get the skin healthy and full of moisture and collagen goes a long way to making us look brighter also.
