Facial Slimming

A masculine jawline is not for everybody

One unique method of achieving a slimmer facial structure, is to use botulinum toxin to our advantage by placing it in the masseter muscles. These are the thick, strong muscles used to help with chewing, which can be felt at the corner of your jaw on either side. Chew down while touching these and you can see how much volume they add.

While it does give a defined jawline which is suited to certain facial types, those who want a narrower V shape, or a loveheart shaped face opt to reduce their masseters, while sacrificing their ability to chew overcooked steak (then you have an even more valid reason to order medium rare).

What is it?

Not to sound too technical or geeky, but we achieve controlled atrophy of the masseter muscle by placing botulinum toxin directly to the part of the muscle we want to reduce. Lucky for us, we have many muscles that control chewing, so we can still eat just fine.

Who’s it for?

Best for people who can feel that added muscular bulk in the jaw. To check if yours does, look in the mirror, place the pad of your index and middle fingers just above the two corners of the jaw, and chew down. Watch them move considerably.

It isn’t best for people who have minimal muscle, but very sharp bone structure, as it might not get the desired look. We can turn to fillers to achieve the same result here instead.

What’s the procedure?

While you look in the handheld mirror, your doctor will ask you to clench and relax your jaw, feeling for where the muscle protrudes the most and is the strongest. We’ll mark about 6 spots to inject toxin into. This is the most comfortable area to treat of all, and gives such a dramatic improvement it’s hard to believe not everyone has had it.
