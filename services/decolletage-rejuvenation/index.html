Décolletage Rejuvenation

The area that transitions neck to the chest is referred to as the décolletage area. Whilst it may not be an area that usually receives much attention in the aesthetic industry, it is an important area for skin rejuvenation as it receives a considerable amount of sun exposure and is not exempt from the effects of ageing and gravity. By improving skin hydration and skin quality in this area, there can be a dramatic improvement in fine lines, wrinkles and colour.

What is it?

Rejuvenation of the décolletage area involves the placement of hydration complexes in the form of Aqua-shine, PRP, or both, combined to improve skin quality.

Who’s it for?

For anyone who feels their skin in this area is beginning to show signs of ageing with thinning skin, or a crepe like appearance, or wrinkling. Often this is a very neglected area with most people focusing on the face. However if the two do not match well in skin health and appearance, it can be a tell-tale sign of our skin age.

What’s the procedure?

First the skin is prepared with a topical anaesthetic cream to help numb the area. Then we sterilise the skin and use our electronic injecting device to place Aqua-shine, PRP or a combination of the two into the skin at the chosen depths.

After we place the formulation into the skin there is an instant hydration boost, which then improves over the next few weeks. For optimal effects we recommend three treatments with Aqua-shine between 3-4 weeks apart.

Is there downtime?

If PRP is combined with Aqua-Shine there can be small visible lumps in the skin for 24-72 hours following, but each day generally improves. With PRP on its own the lumps last less than 24 hours in general. There will be some redness, but this is reduced by using a cool compress following the procedure.
