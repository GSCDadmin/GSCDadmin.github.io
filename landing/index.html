About Us

Welcome to GS Cosmetic Doctors (GSCD), co-located at Green Square Health.

The doctors at GSCD can enhance and define your features using the best non-surgical treatments available to date. Our team will often attend leading international conferences in order to bring cutting edge treatments to our clients.

We are a medical cosmetic clinic. This means that your practitioner is a specialist doctor who has experience in all aspects of medicine, with added qualifications and training in cosmetic services. We only supply and stock TGA approved injectable medicines, which means that they have been vigorously tested and passed Australian Government certification to allow their use.
