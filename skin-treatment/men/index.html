Men

“The Devil hath power, to assume a pleasing shape” – William Shakespeare, Hamlet

Although all our procedures mentioned so far are unisex, let’s make it a little easier for those who only want to have a ‘boys’ look’.

Men are stereotypically known for having a rugged look. Often that’s a euphemism for an aged appearance. And as eluded to previously, ageing is the process of losing collagen and hyaluronic acid within the skin, permanent expression lines and sun damage. The basis of treatment for most men is to increase the quality and health of the skin. This is enough to give a vibrancy and healthy glow without any cosmetic changes.

For other men there is a need to recreate some volume changes and improve the damage done. Classical aesthetic teaching for men revolves around sexual dimorphism. That is, there are distinctive features that differ between men and women to enhance what is masculine and what is feminine. And whilst this did base the foundation, we’ve grown beyond this in our application of rejuvenation and beautification when dealing with the male face.

Ageing doesn’t discriminate, and often is harsher to those who didn’t get the basic foundations of skincare growing through their formative years. Sunshine, rugged winds, harsh surf, big expressive faces, they all leave their mark.

The focus at GS Cosmetic Doctors is always about improving skin quality. This is the basis of a great, youthful and healthy appearance. Our Aqua-shine and PRP treatments improve the fine lines that come with age, without changing the shape of your face at all.

But for those who want to erase some of their harsher years away, ToxinA and expertly placed dermal fillers can rewind the clock instantly. People can then only wonder why you never look tired.

However if it’s more you want, then more we can give. Facial slimming, enhancing the jawline, adding balance and volume to lips, noses, chins and cheeks, filling in dark under eye hollows and fixing acne scars is what we do. So let us do it for you so you too can have great skin.
